Transforming Healthcare

We love solving the hard problems that no one else wants to tackle. We embrace polyglot programming, distributed systems,and open source. We also know that it’s important to allow engineers to take a step back and try new things. That’s why we host periodic hackathons, meetups, tech talks, and an annual internal two-day developers conference in addition to attending , sponsoring, and speaking at external conferences.

At Cerner, we use the latest technologies to transform healthcare. We are the engineers and designers that focus on making you, your family, and friends healthy and happy.

Share the Love

We attend a lot of conferences. As of Nov 2014, we send around 9% of our engineers to conferences each year. We have also started sponsoring conferences in order to get Cerner’s name out in the industry. Cerner engineers are also getting their talks accepted at conferences. We feel so strongly about Cerner engineers speaking at conferences that if your talk is accepted at a conference, we’ll pay your way to go!

DevCon is Cerner’s internal 2-day engineering conference. It’s an opportunity for us to take time off from day-to-day work and share knowledge, get to know others, and have fun. In 2014, we had 270+ talk submissions for just 90 slots, 3000 attendees, and two great keynotes.

Check out the videos at youtube.com/user/CernerEng

Today, open source is the core technology behind many of our architectures and projects. As of November 2014, Cerner has 5 repositories in our Github organization. Cerner is also a silver sponsor of the Apache Software Foundation (higher than Twitter!). We want to acknowledge our commitment to open source and support organizations like the ASF which we benefit from.

These are in addition to a variety of developer centric meetups around the KC area. Some Cerner-sponsored and some even led by Cerner engineers. Check out our calendar to find out where you can attend both.
