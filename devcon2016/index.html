Instructions

The DevCon theme this year is a mystery! Each day this week you will be presented with a puzzle to solve. These puzzles are clues that will help you solve the mystery. Each day a new puzzle will become available until the last day when the theme is revealed. Solve the day’s puzzle, earn a prize! Solve the puzzles every day, earn a bonus prize! Select day 1 to see the first puzzle!

How to play

Each day you will be presented with a quote from a movie and an empty phrase. You will need to construct this phrase with letters from the quote. A key will tell you where to place the letters from the quote in the completed the phrase.

Each number in the key represents a corresponding letter in the movie quote. Using the number in the first blank, count the letters in the movie quote until you’ve reached that letter. (Spaces and punctuation do not count - only letters). Once you have found the letter, insert it in the blank in place of the number. Continue decoding the numbers until the blanks are filled with the corresponding letters. For example: If the quote was “Use the force” from Star Wars and the first blank had the number 4 in it, the correct letter to place in the first blank would be “T” as it is the fourth letter in the quote.

Once you have identified the Movie and the phrase, go to the Innovations, Continuous, or Malvern Corp III Cafe between 1:30 - 3:00 pm (local time). Find the DevCon table and tell the volunteer the Movie and phrase. They will give you a ticket that you can redeem for a pack of 3 posters on Friday. Check back on this site each day for the next puzzle.
